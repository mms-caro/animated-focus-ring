Animated focus ring

This page builds upon the WICG :focus-ring polyfill by adding support for animating the focus ring as it moves around the page via a simple drop-in script.

The behavior is based on the behavior of the focus ring on the WebAIM website.

This can be useful for keyboard users who might have a difficult time seeing where their focus ring has moved to when they press tab, which can be particularly hard if focusable elements on the page are spaced far apart.
